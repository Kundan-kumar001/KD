Top 3 things to do at Rohtas

Sher Shah Suri's tomb

It is a remarkable ex. of Afghan architecture. It was built in the middle of an artificial lake, showcasing a unique design that blends Persian and Indian styles.

Tutla Bhawani Waterfall

It's a popular spot for nature lovers, offering opportunities for photography, trekking, and religious visits

Manjhar Kund

It's known for being a popular spot for picnics and hiking, and is especially vibrant during the monsoon season.
